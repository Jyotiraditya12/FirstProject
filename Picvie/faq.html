01. How to create Picvie Albums on a smartphone/tab (using existing digital content)?Top

  1. Open Picvie on your Smartphone/Tab.
  2. Click on "Build album" icon.
  3. To start, select layout for your page (1 photo, 2 photos, 3 photos, 4 photos or 9 photos per page).
  4. Click "Memory" icon to find all digital media folders on your Smartphone/tab (alternatively, click "Albums" icon to find all digital media used in creating Picvie album.)
  5. Click on "Pictures", click on the pictures you want to add, see pictures being added to the layout.
  6. If you want to add additional content, select "Yes" in the dialog box that opens or "No" to proceed.
  7. Click on "Videos" folder, and click on the video you want to add.
  8. Click on "Music" folder, click on the audio you want to add.
  9. To Add Weblinks, Press and Hold (Long Press) on the page being created, type in or paste the URL you wish to add, click Ok to continue.
  10. To "Add Comment" click on the page being created, use keypad to add content, select "OK" to continue.
  11. Check the info bar to confirm the number of Audios, Videos and Pictures added to the page are reflected correctly.
  12. Click on "Next Page", continue with the above steps to create subsequent pages.
  13. Once you have created all the pages, click on "Save Album".
  14. "Name the Album" and "Select Theme" by clicking on an appropriate theme in the Drop Down menu.
  15. Press "OK", Bingo! You have just created your first Picvie Album.
  16. Scroll by Album name in "My Albums" to view the Album.


02. How to change a Theme of an existing Picvie Album?Top

  1. Tap and hold on the Album for which you want to change theme.
  2. Select "Change Theme" in the dialog box.
  3. Select appropriate theme from the drop down menu. The theme of the Album will have changed.
  4. Scroll by Album name in "My Albums" to view the Album.


03. How to delete an existing Picvie Album?Top

  1. Tap and hold on the Album which you want to delete.
  2. Select "Delete Album" in the dialog box. Select "Yes" to delete.
  3. The Album is now deleted.


04. How to password protect an existing Picvie Album?Top

  1. Tap and hold on the Album which you want to protect.
  2. Select "Protect Album" in the dialog box.
  3. Select "OK" in the dialog box "That password hasn't been set".
  4. Choose "Password for locking albums". Input password using the keypad.
  5. Select "Save".
  6. Your Album is now Password Protected.
  7. To confirm, check for the "lock" icon on the protected Album.


05. How to unprotect an Album?Top

  1. Tap and hold on the Album which you want to password unprotect.
  2. Select "Protect Album" in the dialog box. Select "Yes" in the dialog box "Do you want to unprotect the Album?"
  3. Input the password which was used to protect the Album.
  4. Select "Save".
  5. Your Album is now Unprotected.
  6. To confirm, check to see the "lock" icon has disappeared from the unprotected Album.


06. How to create Picvie Albums while clicking photos? Top

  1. Option 1 – Create new album.
    1. Open Picvie on your Smartphone/Tab.
    2. Click "Camera" icon.
    3. Name the Album and select "Save".
    4. Start and continue to click photos while saving it.
    5. Done? Now press back button (on your Smartphone/Tab)
    6. View the just created Album in "My Albums".
  2. Option 2 – Add pictures to an existing album.
    1. Click on "Add Photos to Album" icon.
    2. Select Album to which you want to add Photos.
    3. Start and continue to click photos for the Album, remember to save.
    4. Done? Now press back button (on your Smartphone/Tab).
    5. View the just clicked photos in the selected Album in "My Albums".


07. How to view Albums?Top

  1. Open Picvie on your Smartphone/Tab.
  2. Tap on the Album you want to view by scrolling through the Albums in "My Albums".
  3. Check to see if any of the Icons in the Menu bar is highlighted/glowing.
  4. Press on (additional photos icon) to view additional photos connected with the page.
  5. Press on (Video icon) to watch video connected with the page.
  6. Press on (web icon) icon to view web page connected with the page.
  7. Press on (Facebook icon) to post the page on FB.
  8. To view EXIF data, long press on the image.
  9. Swipe or use forward/backward button to move to next page.
  10. You have reached the end of the Album, if you reach the Picvie page.
  11. If at any point, you want to exit the Album, use "Back" button (on your Smartphone/Tab) to go to "My Albums".


08. How to view Picvie album as a slideshow?Top

  1. Open Picvie on your Smartphone/Tab.
  2. Tap on the Album you want to view by scrolling through the Albums in "My Albums".
  3. Click on the Options button on the Smartphone/Tab
  4. Click "Autoplay".
  5. To stop the slideshow, click on the Back button (on your Smartphone/Tab).


09. How to build a Picvie album using PC Builder?Top

  1. Launch Picvie on your PC by clicking on it.
  2. Click on "Picvie Explorer".
  3. Click on the "Pictures" Folder, drag and drop the pictures you want to use for the album into a stack (box on the right hand side).
  4. When you drop a photo for the first time, you will be prompted to "Name the Album".
  5. Name the Album and press "Save to continue, continue to add photos to the stack.
  6. Click on "Videos" folder, drag and drop the Videos you want to use for the album into the stack.
  7. Click on "Audios" folder, drag and drop the Audios you want to use for the album into the stack.
  8. Now you have all the materials to create your Album.
  9. Click on "Create an Album" which is displayed on the stack.
  10. Chose the layout you want to use for the page (1 photo, 2 photo, 3 photos, 4 photos or 9 photos per page).
  11. Select the theme for the album (Colors, Birthday, Party, Indian Wedding, Wedding, Windows 8 Blue, Windows 8 Pink, Glass, Desert and Pro)
  12. Drag and drop photos into the layout.
  13. Drag and drop additional content like audios/videos in the additional content box.
  14. Add Weblink by clicking "Link from Web", copy and paste URL into "Enter URL" box, select "OK".
  15. Watch for Web Icon in additional content area.
  16. Select "Text Placement" to choose the position of the Text (top right, top left or bottom).
  17. Click on "Next Page", continue with the above steps to create subsequent pages.
  18. Once you have created all the pages, click on "Save Album".
  19. Bingo! You have just created your first Picvie Album.


10. How to Publish a Picvie Album from the PC builder to a smartphone/Tab?Top

  1. Connect Smartphone/Tab to PC using an USB cable.
  2. On the Device -> Go to "settings", click on "Developer Options", select "USB Debugging".
  3. On the PC, Launch Picvie, select "Albums & Archives", click on "Unpublished Albums", select Album to publish, press "Publish Picvie".
  4. Scroll by Album name in "My Albums" to view the Album.

12. How to Archive stacks to create Picvie Albums later?Top

  1. Launch Picvie on your PC by clicking on it.
  2. Click on "Picvie Explorer".
  3. Click on the "Pictures" Folder, drag and drop the pictures you want to use for the album into a stack (box on the right hand side).
  4. When you drop a photo for the first time, you will be prompted to "Name the Album".
  5. Name the Album and press "Save"; continue to add photos to the stack.
  6. Click on "Videos" folder, drag and drop the Videos you want to use for the album into the stack.
  7. Click on "Audios" folder, drag and drop the Audios you want to use for the album into the stack.
  8. Now you have all the materials to create your Album.
  9. Once you have completed selecting all the materials to create an Album and want to continue later.
  10. Drag the entire stack to Archive icon.
  11. Click on "Album and Archive Viewer" tab to find the stack.

Edit/create new Album by using steps as mentioned here.

13. What's a stack?Top
A collection of all digital media which will be used to create an Album. Using a stack you can create an Album or archive it for later use.

14. What is a Layout?Top
Layout represents number of photos you can add on a particular page. Picvie offers 1 photo, 2 photos, 3 photos, 4 photos and 9 photos layout.

15. What is a Theme?Top
Is a particular setting or ambience represented through cons and Background. Picvie offers 10 different theme; Colors, Birthday, Party, Indian Wedding, Wedding, Windows 8 Blue, Windows 8 Pink, Glass, Desert and Pro.
